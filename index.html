About Us

Passionate storytellers capturing life's most precious moments

Our Story

Founded in 2015, Gayan Dissanayake Photography began with a simple vision: to capture the beauty and emotion of life's most important celebrations.

We believe every couple has a unique story to tell. Our team preserves every laugh, tear, and tender moment with care and creativity.

With 500+ weddings captured, we blend cinematic style with timeless elegance that our clients love.
